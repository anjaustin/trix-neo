## The Honest Claims

### What this IS:

- **A hardware-efficient architecture** for deterministic inference
- **A metric space model** where Hamming distance does routing
- **A verifiable system** — frozen shapes are provable
- **Related to Hyperdimensional Computing** — shares DNA, specialized differently
- **Optimized for trust** — safety-critical, auditable, reproducible

### What this IS NOT:

- **A replacement for all ML** — encoding layer may still need traditional methods
- **Zero learning** — XOR accumulation is learning, just not gradient-based
- **Continuous** — fundamentally discrete, continuous approximation is possible
- **Proven at scale** — empirical validation needed

### What this NEEDS:

- **Encoding methodology** — how to get good signatures
- **Composition patterns** — how shapes chain for complex tasks
- **Training validation** — prove XOR accumulation works empirically
- **Scaling architecture** — beyond 512 bits, beyond 30 shapes
- **Benchmarks** — demonstrate value on real tasks
